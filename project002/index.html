History

Steinway & Sons Founded

- Henry S`teinway founded Steinway & Sons in New York.
-This marked the beginning of one of the most renowned piano manufacturing companies in the world.

First Gold Medal

- Just two years after producing their first piano, Steinway & Sons won a gold medal at the Paris Exposition.
- This was the first time a n American-made piano received such an honor at the prestigious event.
- The award established Steinway & Sons' reputation for excellence early on.

Death of Henry Steinway

- Henry Steinway passed away, but his legacy continued through his sons.
- His children took over the company's operations, ensuring the continuity and growth of the business.
- This transition marked the beginning of the second generation of Steinway leadership.

Technological Innovations

- During the 1880s, Steinway & Sons introduced that significantly improved piano techniques.
- These advancements included new designs and improvements in the construction of pianos.
- The innovations helped Steinway & Sons maintain their position as a leader in the industry.

50th Anniversary

- In celebration of their 50th anniversary, Steinway & Sons produced their 100,000th piano.
- The event was a testament to the brand's dedication to quality and craftsmanship.
